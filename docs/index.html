The making of the map.

The US FDA publishes inspection records through their data dashboard (https://datadashboard.fda.gov/ora/cd/inspections.htm). This map shows some locations that have a biological product type and cite tissues, cells, or genes as their product type. This list is not comprehensive.
The CDC published statistic on disease, including arthritis, by county. The map shows the crude prevalence of arthritis in each county as a percentage of population. Red counties have a higher prevalence of arthritis than white counties. The highest prevalence is 42% of the county population.
